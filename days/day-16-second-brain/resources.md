# Day 16 — Build Your Second Brain in Obsidian

## Tools used in the video

- **Obsidian** — https://obsidian.md — Free, local, open-source knowledge base. Every note is a plain markdown file, which is exactly why Claude can read and write against it.
- **Obsidian Terminal plugin** — https://obsidian.md/plugins?search=terminal — Adds a terminal pane inside Obsidian so you can run `claude` against your vault without leaving the app. <!-- verify URL -->
- **Agent Files plugin** — community plugin for managing agents, skills, commands, and Claude conversations in one place inside Obsidian. <!-- Samin, verify exact plugin name/URL -->
- **Claude Desktop** — https://claude.com/download — Used here to scaffold the evolving brain template into your new vault.
- **Excalidraw plugin** — bundled with the template; gives you hand-drawn diagrams inside Obsidian.
