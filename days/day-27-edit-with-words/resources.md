## Downloads / templates for this day

VibeFrame clipping prompt — drop your video in first, then paste:

```
Hey, I'm trying to make highlights for this video.

I want a bunch of different clips that I can make into standalone videos
that answer specific questions — clipped from this video.

For example, here's a channel that does this well: [link to Hormozi-style
highlights channel]. He takes long videos, clips them up, and makes smaller
videos. I want you to do the same for the video I attached.

Make the clips, put them in a folder, and then show me.
```

Hyperframes reel prompt — drop one clip in first, then paste:

```
Hey, can you install Hyperframes.

With Hyperframes, take this clip and make it into a vertical Instagram reel.

Put captions on the bottom — transcribe the video and put the captions there.
On the top, put a hook: [YOUR HOOK LINE].

Render it and show me.
```
