Key takeaways

  • Three models, one rule: Sonnet by default, Opus when you need deeper thinking, Haiku for bulk or speed.
  • 80% of this course runs on the Claude Desktop app. Download it today.
  • Memory + artifacts must be on. That's non-negotiable for the rest of the course.
  • Pro plan is fine to start. Don't jump to Max until you're hitting limits.
  • Voice input (Whisperflow or the built-in voice button) is way faster than typing — and you'll think clearer out loud.
