## Why this matters

Most people are using about 10% of what Claude can actually do. They open it, type a message, get an answer, close the tab. Meanwhile, other people are running entire businesses on top of it — landing clients, building income streams, replacing work they used to hate. That's the gap this 28-day course closes.

## Step-by-step

1. **Watch the 2-minute intro.** Don't skim. This is the map for the next 28 days.
2. **Meet Alex.** He's a marketing manager with too much to do and not enough hours in the day. He starts exactly where you might be right now. By the end of the course, he's automated most of his job, built a side business selling these systems to other companies, and has agents running around the clock for him.
3. **Understand the shape.** The course runs through 10 levels. Each one builds on the last. You'll go from typing your first prompt to running a full AI operating system you can control from your phone.
4. **Commit to building alongside.** Reading or watching isn't enough. Every day has one small action — do it. That's how the results show up.
5. **Skip around if you want, but start from the beginning.** There are skills, hacks, and Easter eggs buried throughout that compound as you go. If you already know some of this, use the chapters — but start from Day 1.

## Common stuck points

- **Trying to watch the whole course in one sitting.** This is built for 15–20 minutes a day. The compounding only works if you build alongside, day by day.
- **Skipping the Alex story beats.** The story is how every concept lands. If you jump to the technical bits, you lose the "why."
- **Thinking you need to be technical.** You don't. If you can type a sentence, you can do this course.

## Key takeaways

- You're not here to learn Claude trivia. You're here to build a system that replaces the work you hate.
- Most people use 10% of Claude. The gap between that and running your whole operation is what you're closing over the next 28 days.
- Alex's story is the spine of the course — every concept becomes real through what he builds.
